Visa and Travel

Please be aware that if you are the holder of a Chinese passport, you must apply for a Danish visa before being able to enter Denmark.
This can be done by either applying online on the danish by clicking here. Or by visiting a Danish consulate or embassy.
The Danish embassy in Beijing can be found on the map below this paragraph.

Safety when traveling

It should be noted that Denmark is generally a safe country, however you must be cautious and trust your gut feeling.
Furthermore police will never directly ask you for cash, or any other form of payment. Just like other countries
Denmark also has scam artists. It's advised to use caution when talking to people approaching you.

Payment and currency

Denmark uses the Danish Krone(DKK) as their currency, this means that Euros will not be accepted in Denmark.
DKK can be purchased with a variety of foreign currency in multiple places in Denmark.
However you will most likely not need this as the preferred payment method in Denmark is card.
Most common cards like Mastercard, Visa and American express are accepted however China Unionpay is also accepted.
